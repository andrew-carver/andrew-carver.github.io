About HORiZON

At Horizon, we look to what we believe the purpose of photography is: to inspire. We aim to hire talented artists that have a very clear eye and passion for their work and it shows in their photos.

Our website features work from the top photographers around the world. We make it easy to hire any of these photographers for photoshoots or specific photographed landmarks from whatever regions they are located in. Photo prints  from these artists are also available to purchase on our free app.
